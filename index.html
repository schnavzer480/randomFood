How to use the Restraurant Generator

The Restaurant Generator is the tool you need when you and your SO can't decide what to eat. Just click the button and it will randomly choose a restaurant for you.

McDonald's, KFC, Burger King, Max Hamburgare, ChopChop and "pizza" are already preset in the application. If you want to add a restaurant of your own to the pool, just enter its name in the field above the "Add Food" button

This site is optimized for smartphones. It works on both tablets and larger monitors as well but I would rather be force fed paprika than writing media queries for laptops.
